Amazing Authors

These are the amazing authors whose books we are selling. We are truly lucky to be reading the works of these incredible people

Arthur C Doyle

Sir Arthur Ignatius Conan Doyle was a British writer and physician. He created Sherlock Holmes in 1887 for A Study in Scarlet, the first of four novels about Holmes and Dr. Watson.

Bram Stoker

Abraham Stoker was an Irish author, best known for his 1897 Gothic horror novel Dracula. During his lifetime, he was better known as the assistant and the business manager of the reputed Lyceum Theatre

Edgar Allan Poe

Edgar Allan Poe was an American writer and poet. Poe is best known for his poetry and his tales of mystery. He is regarded as a central figure of Romanticism in American literature.

Herman Melville

Herman Melville was an American novelist of the American Renaissance period. He is the author of Moby-Dick, which is now considered one of the great American novels.

H.G. Wells

Herbert George Wells was an English writer who was prolific in many genres and wrote dozens of novels and short stories. His work also included two books on recreational war games.

Jane Austen

Jane Austen was an English novelist known primarily for her six major novels, which interpret, critique and comment upon the British landed gentry at the end of the 18th century.

Louisa May Alcott

Louisa May Alcott was an American novelist, short story writer, and poet best known as the author of the novel Little Women (1868) and its sequels Little Men (1871) and Jo's Boys (1886).

Mary Shelly

Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley was an English novelist who wrote the Gothic novel Frankenstein; or, The Modern Prometheus (1818), which is considered an early example of science fiction.

Scott F Fitzgerald

Francis Scott Key Fitzgerald was an American novelist, essayist and short story writer best known for his novels depicting the flamboyance and excess of the Jazz Ageâ€”a term he popularized.

Solomon Northup

Solomon Northup was an American abolitionist and the primary author of the memoir Twelve Years a Slave. He was the son of a freed slave and a free woman of color.
